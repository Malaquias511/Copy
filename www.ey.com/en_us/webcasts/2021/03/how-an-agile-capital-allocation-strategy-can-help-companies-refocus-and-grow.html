        How an agile capital allocation strategy can help companies refocus and grow

        In this webcast, EY and industry leaders will discuss how to develop an agile capital allocation strategy and prioritize investments, particularly as a result of pandemic-related business disruptions.

        Related topics Capital allocation Strategy and Transactions Strategy by EY-Parthenon CFO agenda

        Pandemic-related market disruption has caused many companies to rapidly revise their capital allocation plan. Many companies may now need to refocus on their core business, while also recharging growth through M&A, digital investments and other ways to meet customer needs. A company’s capital allocation approach should align with its value creation goals and long-term strategy, while also allowing it to pivot during disruptions. 

        During this webcast, industry and EY leaders will discuss:

        • Capital allocation priorities for 2021 and beyond
        • The pandemic’s implications on capital allocation
        • What factors enable or stand in the way of effective investments
        • How companies are incorporating environmental, social and governance concepts into the decision-making process

        Speakers:

        • Dev Ahuja, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Novelis Inc.
        • Loren Garruto, EY Global and Americas Corporate Finance Leader, Principal, Ernst & Young LLP
        • Ben Hoban, Senior Manager, Strategy and Transactions, Ernst & Young LLP

        Moderator:

        • Evan Sussholz, Partner, Strategy and Transactions, Ernst & Young LLP
